== Description ==

The **NinjaTeam Click to Chat plugin** supports multiple chat apps and messaging platforms, making communication easier for your customers.

With this WP Support All-in-One Floating Widget Plugin, you can create a click-to-chat button that appears in the corner of your website. When clicked or tapped, this button expands to display a selection of chat apps so your customers can choose their preferred method of communication. It works great on both desktop and mobile devices.
